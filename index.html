2018 Annual KAT Swim Medley

Proceeds donated to Kimmy, Anna and Torri; The broke girls of 517 Evergreen.

The annual KAT swim medley will take place on October 7th at the IM West Pool. Racers are required to check in by 12-Noon and the medley will start promptly at 2PM. The Medley relay is swum by four different swimmers each swimming one stroke.
